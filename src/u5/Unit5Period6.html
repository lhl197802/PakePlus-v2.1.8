My dear friends,

Each of us must obey public rules. Let's say no to the bad behaviour.

In our daily life, we can see some bad behaviour around us. For example, some people drop litter everywhere in public places. Others jump the queue when waiting for buses or in supermarkets. Some even talk loudly on the phone in libraries or museums. These actions are not only impolite but also affect others.

As students, we should set a good example. We must remember to wait in line, keep quiet in public places, and put rubbish into bins. When we see bad behaviour, we can politely remind others to follow the rules.

Let's work together to make our society more polite and pleasant. Thank you!

Yang Qi
