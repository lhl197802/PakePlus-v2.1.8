The Changes in My Hometown

In the past, people in my hometown lived a poor life. The houses were old and small. Pollution was very serious, and there was rubbish everywhere. The traffic was not convenient and roads were narrow, so few visitors came here.

Now great changes have taken place in my hometown. The houses are large and bright. Many people have their own cars. The roads are wide and clean. The rivers have become much clearer and the sky has been bluer. Every year, thousands of people come to visit my hometown.

I think we should take an active part in protecting the environment. I hope my hometown will become more and more beautiful in the future. There will be more tourists coming to my hometown.
