Save the Earth: Let's Do Our Part!

The earth is the only home for everyone on it. It's crying for our help now. As middle school students, we have the power to make a difference and help save the earth!

To protect the earth, we can start by saving water, growing more trees, and reducing pollution. We can also save animals and recycle whenever possible.

We can also choose to ride a cycle instead of taking a taxi to help stop pollution, and make sure to always throw rubbish in the proper bins. By doing our part, we can make a positive impact on the environment and create a better future for ourselves and generations to come.
