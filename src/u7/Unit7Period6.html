My Volunteering Experience During Summer Vacation

Volunteering is very meaningful and important. It can help others and make us feel happy too.

I still remember one of my volunteering experiences during this summer vacation clearly. I went to an old people's home with my friends. We helped clean the rooms and chatted with the elderly. Some of them told us stories about their youth. We also performed songs and dances for them. They were very happy and smiled all the time.

From this experience, I learned that a small act of kindness can make a big difference. I felt proud that I could bring joy to others. I will continue to volunteer in the future.
