The following suggestions will help you if you have some of these worries.

Work with an experienced volunteer. He/She volunteered a lot and he/she can tell you a lot about the volunteer work and help you get used to it quickly.

Invite a friend or family member to serve. It will be a good experience to volunteer with him/her.

Finally, it is important to enjoy the volunteer experience and to remember that not all volunteer experiences are perfect. If one experience isn't good enough, don't give up and you'll surely find the right chance to enjoy yourself. Spend some time on volunteer work and you will see great changes in people's lives.
