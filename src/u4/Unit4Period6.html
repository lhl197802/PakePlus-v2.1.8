This book is like a friend who encourages me to dream big. Every time I read it, I find new ideas. It's not just a storyâ€”it's a key to imagination.

My Favourite Book

My favourite book is The Old Man and the Sea. It is a short novel written by Ernest Hemingway. I got this book from my school library last month and I have read it twice.

The book tells a story about an old fisherman named Santiago. He fights with a giant fish for three days in the sea. Although he loses the fish to sharks at last, he never gives up. His spirit is very moving.

I like this book because it shows us the importance of courage and perseverance. The old man's words "A man can be destroyed but not defeated" really touches me. Whenever I face difficulties, I think of him and feel strong again. This book is not just a story; it is a lesson about life. I highly recommend it to everyone.
