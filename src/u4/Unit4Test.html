Mibs Beaumont is the main character of the book. Her grandpa can move mountains, one of her brothers can cause hurricane, and another brother creates electricity. She can't wait to turn 13 because she wants to see what power she'll get.

But just before her big birthday, her father gets hurt in a terrible accident and cannot wake up. Suddenly, Mibs' hope of having X-ray vision is gone. All she wants is magic to save her dad.

With her brothers and some friends, she decides to see her father in the hospital. They secretly get on a bus. Along the way, they make some friends. More importantly, the Beaumonts learn to control their magic and use it to help others.
